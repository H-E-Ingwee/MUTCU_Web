About the Integral Ministry

The Integral Ministry is a unique and cross-cutting ministry within MUTCU, designed to work in conjunction with all other Union ministries to enhance the transformative gospel. Its core focus is on developing and implementing creative and innovative ways to spread the gospel, ensuring that MUTCU's message is relevant and impactful in a dynamic world.

This ministry seeks to integrate faith with all aspects of life, encouraging members to live out their Christian values in their academic, social, and professional spheres. It plays a crucial role in fostering holistic growth and outreach within the MUTCU community.

Key Roles & Activities

The Integral Ministry fosters innovation and holistic gospel outreach.

  • Collaborating with all other MUTCU ministries to enhance gospel outreach.
  • Developing creative and innovative strategies for spreading the gospel.
  • Exploring new avenues and technologies for ministry and evangelism.
  • Partnering with relevant organizations for financial and material support, subject to Executive Council approval.
  • Ensuring official communication and agreements with partnering organizations.
  • Promoting the integration of faith into all aspects of members' lives.

Innovate for the Gospel!
