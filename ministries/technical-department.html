MUTCU Technical Ministry

Serving the Kingdom Through Sound, Media & Technology

About the Technical Ministry

The Technical Ministry is the backbone of all MUTCU events, ensuring that the message of the gospel is heard and seen clearly. Our dedicated team of technicians and media specialists handles all aspects of sound, lighting, projection, and live streaming for weekly services and special events. We are committed to using technology as a powerful tool for ministry, creating an environment where every member can worship without distraction and every word is delivered with clarity and impact. Our service is behind the scenes but essential to the Union's mission.

Our Areas of Service

Our ministry is made up of different teams working together to deliver excellence.

Sound Ministry

Responsible for all audio aspects, from mixing to microphone setup, ensuring every word of worship is heard with clarity.

MBBC Ministry

Manages all media, broadcasting, and camera operations to capture and project the gospel message visually.

Digital Ministry

Handles the online presence, website management, and digital content creation to reach a broader audience.

Ushering Ministry

Ensures a warm, welcoming, and orderly environment for all members and visitors during services and events.

Operational Policies

Our ministry is guided by MUTCU's official policies to ensure good stewardship and professionalism.

  • Financial Accountability

    In line with MUTCU policies, all technical dockets are required to submit detailed budgets and use official requisition forms for all expenditures. This ensures proper stewardship of the Union's resources.

  • Asset Management & Maintenance

    The ministry adheres to strict policies for the use, borrowing, and maintenance of all our assets, including sound equipment, cameras, and computers. This responsible management ensures the longevity and availability of resources for ministry.

  • Leadership & Transition

    New leaders for the Technical Docket are appointed annually through a formal nomination process, ensuring a smooth and prayerfully guided transition of responsibilities.
