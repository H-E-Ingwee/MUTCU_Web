Creative Arts Ministry (CREAM)

Expressing Faith Through Drama, Dance, Spoken Word & Poetry

About the Creative Arts Ministry (CREAM)

The Creative Arts Ministry (CREAM) provides a lively platform for MUTCU members to express their faith and glorify God through various artistic talents. This includes drama, dance, SPARCS(spoken word, and poetry), Arts, Rap, Creative Writing,Shairi. Our ministry ensures that all creative presentations are biblically aligned and spiritually enriching, serving as a powerful tool for evangelism and edification within the university and beyond.

We believe in nurturing and developing artistic gifts for God's glory, contributing significantly to major Union gatherings and special events like "Creative Night" and collaborations for "Christmas Cantata."

Our Creative Expressions

Within CREAM, various artistic disciplines come together to serve God and inspire the community.

Drama

Our Drama team uses theatrical performances to convey powerful biblical messages, address societal issues from a Christian perspective, and engage audiences emotionally and spiritually. Through skits, plays, and mime, they bring stories to life.

Activities:
  • Weekly rehearsals for upcoming performances.
  • Developing original scripts based on biblical themes or contemporary issues.
  • Performing during Friday fellowships, special events, and outreach programs.
  • Collaborating with other ministries for integrated ministrattions.

Meeting Times: Wednesdays & Thrusdays, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM (Rooom 21)

Dance

The Dance team expresses worship, praise, and spiritual narratives through movement and choreography. Their performances are a visual representation of devotion, designed to inspire, uplift, and minister to the audience.

Activities:
  • Regular practice sessions to choreograph new routines.
  • Performing during worship services, praise fests, and evangelistic events.
  • Participating in inter-CU dance competitions and showcases.
  • Training new members in various dance styles for ministry.

Meeting Times: Saturdays, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Multipurpose Room) - *Specific times confirmed weekly.*

Spoken Word & Poetry

This lively group uses the power of words, rhythm, and vocal delivery to share testimonies, expound on biblical truths, and address contemporary issues from a Christian perspective. Their performances are thought-provoking and spiritually impactful.

Activities:
  • Writing workshops to develop poetic and spoken word pieces.
  • Open mic sessions during "Creative Night" and other fellowship events.
  • Performing during weekly services and special Union gatherings.
  • Mentoring aspiring poets and spoken word artists.

Meeting Times: Thursdays, 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM (CU Office Lounge) - *Specific times confirmed weekly.*

Visual Arts & Design (SPARCS)

While not explicitly detailed as a separate sub-ministry in the provided documents for CREAM, many Creative Arts Ministries include visual aspects. This team focuses on visual storytelling, graphic design, stage decor, and other visual elements that enhance the Union's message and aesthetic during events and communications.

Activities:
  • Designing promotional materials for Union events (posters, social media graphics).
  • Creating stage backdrops and decor for worship services and special events.
  • Developing visual content for presentations and evangelistic campaigns.
  • Photography and videography to document Union activities.

Meeting Times: Wednesdays, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM (Creative Studio) - *Specific times confirmed weekly.*
