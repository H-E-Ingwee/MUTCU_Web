Expressing Faith Through Creativity

About CREAM

The Creative Arts Ministry (CREAM) is a vibrant ministry dedicated to using artistic expression to glorify God and communicate the gospel. We believe that creativity is a divine gift, and we use various mediums like drama, poetry, and visual arts to inspire, edify, and engage the MUTCU community and beyond.

CREAM plays a vital role in our church's outreach and internal events, often collaborating with the Music Ministry to create powerful and memorable worship experiences. Our mission is to raise up a generation of creative worshippers who use their talents for God's kingdom.

Our Creative Outlets

CREAM is a home for all forms of artistic expression. Click on the images to see more!

Drama

The Drama team uses theatrical performances to illustrate biblical truths and explore contemporary issues from a Christian perspective. Their powerful skits and plays are a key component of our creative evangelism efforts.

Activities:
  • Rehearsing and performing skits for weekly fellowships.
  • Developing longer plays for special events like Creative Nights.
  • Using drama as a tool for outreach and missions.

Spoken Word & Poetry

This ministry is for those who love to communicate profound messages through the art of words. Our spoken word artists and poets use their craft to express their faith, encourage the congregation, and minister to souls.

Activities:
  • Sharing original pieces during fellowships.
  • Collaborating with the drama team for integrated performances.
  • Workshops for writing and performance skills.

Visual Arts & Design

The Visual Arts and Design team brings our brand to life through graphic design, illustration, and other visual mediums. They are responsible for creating engaging event posters, social media graphics, and stage designs that enhance our worship spaces.

Activities:
  • Designing graphics for Union events and announcements.
  • Creating visual backdrops for performances.
  • Developing creative installations for the chapel.

Special Creative Events

CREAM is at the forefront of organizing special events that celebrate creativity, including Creative Nights and the annual Christmas Cantata. These events are opportunities to showcase the diverse talents within the ministry and engage the wider student body.

Events:
  • **Creative Nights:** Dedicated evenings for creative expressions.
  • **Christmas Cantata:** A collaborative worship experience with the music ministry.
  • **Creative Experience:** A large-scale event showcasing the best of CREAM.
