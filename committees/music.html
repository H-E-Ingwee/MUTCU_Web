About the Music Ministry

The Music Ministry of MUTCU is dedicated to leading the Union in lively and spiritually uplifting worship experiences. Comprising various teams including the choir, band, and praise teams, this ministry plays a crucial role in creating an atmosphere where members can connect with God through music and song.

Our goal is to develop a heart of worship and praise among the MUTCU family, ensuring that all musical presentations are excellent and align with the Union's spiritual goals. We believe that music is a powerful tool for evangelism, edification, and glorifying God.
