About the Hospitality Ministry

The Hospitality Ministry is the welcoming heart of MUTCU, dedicated to ensuring that all members and visitors feel valued, cared for, and at home within the Union. This ministry is responsible for creating a warm and inviting atmosphere during all gatherings, managing amenities, and providing excellent visitor care.

The Hospitality Committee oversees all activities, from registering and inducting new members into the ministry to preparing budgets and managing programs related to visitor care. Their work ensures that the spirit of fellowship and love is tangible in every MUTCU interaction.

Key Roles & Activities

The Hospitality Ministry ensures a warm and caring environment.

  • Registering and inducting new members into the hospitality ministry.
  • Preparing all hospitality ministry budgets and making budgetary allocations.
  • Overseeing the working of all hospitality programs and visitor care ministries.
  • Ensuring a welcoming atmosphere at all Union events and services.
  • Providing refreshments after meetings that extend beyond three hours.
  • Managing the use, borrowing, and maintenance of Union assets like kitchen utensils.
