About the RMC

The Resource Mobilization Committee (RMC) is a dedicated team tasked with securing the financial and material resources necessary for MUTCU to fulfill its vision and mission. We provide a proactive approach to funding our mission work, evangelism efforts, and leadership development programs. Our work ensures the Union is adequately equipped for all its activities and can sustain its impact within the university and the wider society.

View Our Framework

Operational Framework & Policies

Our committee operates under clear policies to ensure transparency and effectiveness.

Financial Accountability

All funds are handled by the Treasury, with all records open for inspection by the Auditing Committee, ensuring proper stewardship of member offerings and donations.

Ethical Standards

All fundraising methods must be consistent with MUTCU's doctrinal basis and Christian values. We do not engage in coercion, gambling, or manipulation.

Reporting & Authority

The RMC is directly accountable to the Executive Council and must obtain prior approval for any fundraising activity to ensure alignment with the Union's goals.

Our Fundraising Approaches

We employ a variety of ethical methods to secure the necessary resources for our ministries.

  • Love Offerings & Donations

    We accept and manage love offerings from members, partner churches, and alumni to support various Union activities.

  • Merchandise Sales

    The committee oversees the sale of MUTCU branded merchandise as a way to generate funds and promote our identity.

  • Church Visitations

    We coordinate visits to local churches to share about our ministry and appeal for support, fostering a strong network of partners.

Committee Leadership

Our team is guided by a dedicated leader, ensuring strategic and prayerful efforts.
