About CREAM

The Creative Arts Ministry (CREAM) is a vibrant and diverse ministry dedicated to using artistic expression to glorify God and communicate the gospel. We believe that creativity is a divine gift, and we use various mediums like drama, dance, and spoken word to inspire, edify, and engage the MUTCU community and beyond.

CREAM plays a vital role in our union's outreach and internal events, often collaborating with the Music Ministry to create powerful and memorable worship experiences. Our mission is to raise up a generation of creative worshippers who use their talents for God's kingdom.

Our Creative Outlets

CREAM is a home for all forms of artistic expression. Click on the images to see more!

Dance

The Dance ministry uses choreographed movements to praise and worship God, creating a dynamic and expressive form of prayer. Our dancers minister during fellowships and special events, bringing stories to life through motion.

Meeting Time:
  • Weekly Practice: Wednesdays, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Drama

The Drama team uses theatrical performances to illustrate biblical truths and explore contemporary issues from a Christian perspective. Their powerful skits and plays are a key component of our creative evangelism efforts.

Meeting Time:
  • Weekly Rehearsal: Wednesdays and Thursdays, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

SPARCS (Spoken Word, Poetry, Arts)

This ministry is for those who love to communicate profound messages through the art of words and visuals. Our spoken word artists, poets, and visual artists use their craft to express faith and minister to the congregation.

Meeting Time:
  • Weekly Session: Tuesday, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Modelling

The Modelling ministry uses fashion and presentation to exemplify Christian values and modesty. They organize events to showcase God's creativity in form and design, ministering through visual presentation.

Meeting Time:
  • Weekly Session: Wednesdays, 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
