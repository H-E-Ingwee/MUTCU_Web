About the Creative Ministry

The Creative Ministry (CREAM) provides a lively platform for MUTCU members to express their faith and glorify God through various artistic talents. This includes drama, dance, spoken word, poetry, and other forms of creative expression. The ministry ensures that all creative presentations are biblically aligned and spiritually enriching, serving as a powerful tool for evangelism and edification.

Brian Mwaura, as the Creative Ministry Coordinator, leads the development and execution of these artistic endeavors. The ministry organizes special events like "Creative Night" and contributes significantly to major Union gatherings, showcasing the diverse talents within MUTCU.

Key Roles & Activities

The Creative Ministry uses arts to communicate faith.

  • Developing and performing drama, dance, spoken word, and poetry pieces.
  • Organizing and hosting "Creative Night" events to showcase artistic talents.
  • Collaborating with other ministries, such as Music, for combined worship experiences like Christmas Cantata.
  • Ensuring all creative presentations are reviewed for biblical alignment and spiritual impact.
  • Nurturing and developing artistic talents among Union members for ministry.

Unleash Your Creativity for God!

If you have artistic talents, join the Creative Ministry and use them for His glory.
