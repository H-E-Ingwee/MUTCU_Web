About the Technical Ministry

The Technical Ministry is responsible for all technical aspects of MUTCU's activities, ensuring smooth operations during services, events, and online engagements. As per the constitution, it encompasses the ICT Ministry, Publicity Ministry, and Ushering Ministry.

From the policy framework, the ministry includes Sound Ministry, Ushering Ministry, MBBC (Media Broadcasting and Broadcast Committee), and Digital Ministry. The overview highlights asset management and support for worship experiences.
