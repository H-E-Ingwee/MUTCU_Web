About the Hospitality Ministry

The Hospitality Ministry, as outlined in the constitution, focuses on creating a welcoming environment for all MUTCU members and visitors. It includes the Kitchen Ministry and works to foster fellowship through service.

Per the policy framework, the ministry oversees Kitchen Ministry, Guest Relations, and Event Catering, ensuring food safety, resource management, and a spirit of generosity and care in all activities.
